## üí° Potential Startup Ideas Aligned with Our Research

### Idea 1: "CreativeCare Recovery Network"
**Product**: SaaS platform for substance use recovery support tailored to creative professionals

**How it addresses SUD**:
- Peer-to-peer support groups for musicians/artists in recovery
- Flexible meeting times for irregular schedules
- Industry-specific triggers and coping strategies
- Anonymous community (protects career reputation)

**Business Model**: Subscription (freemium + premium mentorship)

**Customer**: Musicians, artists, photographers with SUDs or in recovery

**NIDA Alignment**: Novel delivery channel for SUD treatment support for underserved population

---

### Idea 2: "GigGuard - Financial Stress & Addiction Prevention"
**Product**: Mobile app that combines financial wellness tools with SUD risk screening

**How it addresses SUD**:
- Our research shows financial stress ‚Üí substance use pathway
- Real-time budgeting for irregular creative income
- Automated SUD risk screening when financial stress detected
- Connects to resources BEFORE addiction develops

**Business Model**: B2B2C (partnerships with artist organizations) + direct consumer

**Customer**: Freelance creative professionals, gig economy workers

**NIDA Alignment**: Prevention through addressing root cause (economic precarity)

---

### Idea 3: "VenueWatch - Occupational Health Monitoring"
**Product**: Wearable + app for tracking exposure risks in performance venues

**How it addresses SUD**:
- Tracks noise exposure (tinnitus ‚Üí sleep disruption ‚Üí self-medication)
- Monitors secondhand smoke/substance exposure in venues
- Correlates environment with substance use patterns
- Early intervention alerts

**Business Model**: B2B (venues/organizations) + B2C (individual artists)

**Customer**: Musicians, venue operators, artist unions

**NIDA Alignment**: Prevention via occupational health intervention

---

### Idea 4: "StreamHealth Analytics"
**Product**: AI-powered mental health monitoring via creative output patterns

**How it addresses SUD**:
- Our research links streaming revenue collapse to mental health decline
- Algorithm detects changes in creative output (warning sign)
- Integrates with existing platforms (Spotify, Bandcamp)
- Predicts SUD risk before crisis

**Business Model**: SaaS for artist management companies, labels, platforms

**Customer**: Artist managers, streaming platforms, individual artists

**NIDA Alignment**: Novel early detection tool using digital phenotyping
